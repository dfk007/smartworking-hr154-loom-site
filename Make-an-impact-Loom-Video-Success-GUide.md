**How to Nail Your Video Resume in 3 Simple Steps:**

1. **Start Strong (1 min):**

● **Who are you?** Introduce yourself with energy & confidence.  
● **Why this role?** Show enthusiasm and link the role to your experience.  
● **Your expertise in a nutshell\!** Highlight 2–3 key skills with quick, relevant examples.

2. **Showcase Your Best Work (3 mins):**

**\!If you don’t have content to share, please DO NOT share your SCREEN, just use your camera to record the video\!**  
**Share a strong example of a project or initiative you played a key role in** – from idea to completion – highlighting your impact, problem-solving, and results. If possible, reference work done for a well-known or reputable company, and clearly explain your contribution and outcomes.  
***Cultural tip*** \- In the UK, the term “IT” usually refers to hardware. Use “technology” instead for a broader and more accurate meaning.

3. **Wrap It Up Like a Pro (1 min):**

● Why should we hire you?  
● End with a confident, friendly thank you\!

**Pro Tips for a Standout Video:**

**● Optimize your recording setup:** Use a computer in a quiet space, and test your microphone, camera, and internet connection beforehand.  
 **● Use a professional background or suitable filter, and pay attention to lighting for a clear, polished look.**  
 **● Be natural and engaging** – skip the script, speak with confidence.  
 ● **Look into the camera** – it helps build connection.  
 ● **Don’t show your LinkedIn or resume while explaining your project** – focus on your skills, experience, and how you can contribute.  
 ● **If you can’t share any project,** share a version that’s already public-facing (available to the public) or create a quick demo/mock-up with company sensitive information blurred.

**Remember:** Your Loom video is your chance to show what makes you unique – let your personality shine and make a genuine connection with us\! Please note that the Loom video will also be used to assess your English language proficiency.
