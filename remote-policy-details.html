Smart Working Remote Policy

To be considered further for this role you must read,sign and agree to this “Smart Working Remote Policy” document.

At Smart Working, we embrace a fully remote working environment, allowing you to carry out your responsibilities from the comfort of your home. This allows you to save time and money by eliminating the need for daily commuting, contributing to a more efficient and healthier work-life balance. We firmly believe in the effectiveness of remote work when approached with dedication and focus, ensuring that the quality of output remains on par with traditional office settings.

Here are other employee benefits at Smart Working: working for top US & EU businesses long term, fixed working hours - you will never have to work overtime, no work on weekends, we will provide you with a laptop, full medical insurance provided along with on-time salary payments every month.

As part of your commitment to this role, you will exclusively dedicate 45 hours per week to your responsibilities. The working hours are: 6:30am - 4pm BST (Summer) / 7:30am - 5pm GMT (Winters). You are entitled to a 30 minute break each day.

To maintain transparency and uphold our commitment to clients, Smart Working uses productivity software which tracks activity and hours worked. This system guarantees that Smart Working’s clients receive the agreed-upon working hours. This software is not used to micro manage. Use of remote access software (such as TeamViewer or AnyDesk) on the laptop provided by Smart Working is not possible.

It is a condition of employment at Smart Working that you give your full time & attention during your working hours to Smart Working. Smart Working does not allow dual employment during your working hours. Smart Working does not allow its staff to do contractual work for other companies during your working hours. Smart Working does not allow its staff to outsource their work to third parties.

Smart Working will immediately terminate any employee engaging in dual employment during your working hours, contracting work during your working hours or any employee who is outsourcing their Smart Working work.

It is agreed between Smart Working and you that should our rules on dual employment, contracting and outsourcing of work be broken, you agree to and will recompense Smart Working for three months salary for the damage caused by you to Smart Working and its reputation.

Our robust systems swiftly identify employees who attempt to maintain dual employment during work hours or who attempt to outsource work to third parties. We use state of the art technology which cannot be bypassed.

If you do receive an offer of employment from Smart Working a thorough background verification check will be carried out. This will include checking and proving your identity, address and previous employment record.

You agree to keep the details of your compensation discussion with Smart Working confidential, including your salary expectations and any signing bonus offered. Sharing compensation details with our client(s) is a violation of this agreement and may result in your removal from the current recruitment process, withdrawal of any offer extended, or termination of your contract (if applicable).

We look after our staff very well at Smart Working. We are proud of our low staff turnover and our high Glassdoor rating. Please take a look for yourself. We wish you good luck in finding a position with Smart Working and hope that we will have a long and mutually fruitful relationship with you for many years.

By affixing your signature to this agreement, you agree to the above conditions.
